MEET FOUNDER CANDICE JOHNSON

EARLY YEARS

Candice grew up in Atlanta, GA with 5 siblings. Both parents worked long hours and left the kids at home to fend for themselves. Candice stepped up from an early age to take care of the family and help around the house. Learning a variety home economic and life skills out of necessity. She became good at sewing to stitch up tears in shirts and darn socks to save the family money.

PUBLIC RELATIONS

An alumni of Georgia State University Candice has built a career in Public Relations. Founder and President of a private consulting firm over the last two decades that proudly serves the needs of a diverse client base.

POSTIVE DIRECTION

Positive Direction grew out of the habits and processes that made Canice's life a success. As she raised her son she used a lot of the same tools and people were quickly taking notice. She saw need in the community for positive resources and direction for youth and adults alike and Positive Direction was born.
